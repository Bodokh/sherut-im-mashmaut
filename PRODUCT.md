# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

The Hebrew-speaking Israeli audience of the nonprofit (עמותה) **שירות עם משמעות**:

- Young people approaching meaningful (military / national / civilian) service, and their families.
- Supporters and potential donors who want to back the mission.
- Partner organizations, schools, pre-army academies, and institutions weighing collaboration.
- People seeking the organization's lectures, guidance, and support.

Context: arriving from search, social, a shared video, or a talk they attended; on mobile first and desktop; reading **Hebrew (RTL)** primarily, with **English (LTR)** as a secondary language.

## Product Purpose

שירות עם משמעות ("Service with Meaning") is an Israeli nonprofit that **inspires meaningful service and cultivates values and identity**. It works through women lecturers who give talks, real stories and videos from the field, conferences and events, partnerships, and personal accompaniment & support (ליווי וסיוע).

The site exists to communicate the mission with impact, build trust, surface the lecturers and their stories, and convert visitors into one concrete action: **donate, invite a lecture, partner, or get in touch.** Success = a visitor *feels* the meaning, trusts the organization, and takes a step.

## Brand Personality

Bold, mission-driven, and proudly **Israeli / Zionist (כחול־לבן)**. Warm yet impressive — heartfelt and human, with gravitas and ambition rather than a soft charity template. Voice: clear, purposeful, dignified, hopeful.

Three words: **meaningful · rooted · uplifting.** Emotional goals: pride, belonging, hope, and the pull to act.

## Anti-references

- Generic nonprofit / charity template (stock-photo hero + three identical icon cards + clutter).
- Cold corporate / SaaS sterility (blue-gray, impersonal).
- Bureaucratic / governmental density.
- **Flag-kitsch** — literal, cheap blue-white flag clichés. The Israeli identity is carried with depth (techelet gravitas, Hebrew heritage typography, real field imagery), never stripes-and-stars decoration.
- Editorial-magazine reflex (display serif + italic drop caps + ruled columns + no imagery).

## Design Principles

1. **Meaning is the message.** Every section earns its place by deepening purpose; lead with people and story, not features.
2. **Rooted, not kitsch.** Carry the Israeli/Zionist identity through techelet depth, Hebrew heritage type, and real imagery — never flag-cliché.
3. **Warm gravitas.** Impressive and dignified, but human and warm; faces and stories over abstractions.
4. **Fast first, then cinematic.** Above the fold is instant; motion is reserved for earned, lazy-loaded moments below — choreography, not reflexive fade-rise on every section.
5. **One clear action per fold.** Bold, single-purpose viewports that move the visitor toward donate / invite / partner / contact.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 **AA** target. Full **Hebrew RTL + English LTR** via logical CSS properties and correct `dir` / `lang`. Body contrast ≥ 4.5:1, large text / UI ≥ 3:1, verified against the techelet palette. Keyboard-navigable with visible focus, semantic landmarks, accessible names. `prefers-reduced-motion` honored with crossfade/instant fallbacks for every animation. Touch targets ≥ 44px. Zoom to 200% without breakage.
